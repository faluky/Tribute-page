MAMA AMINA

Communiy leader, mentor and a true local hero

Biography

Mama Amina has dedicated over 25 years to improving women's education in our town . She founded a community center that provides free classes for girs and women of all ages.

Achievements

  • Started a free literacy programs for women.
  • Helped more than 500 young girls attend secondary school.
  • Honoured with the NationalCommunity Service Award.

Quotes

"Mama Amina taught me to believe in myself. She gave me the courage to finish high school." - Students
"Her kindness and dedication changed our community." - Neighbours
